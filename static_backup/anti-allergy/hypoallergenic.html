Product Details

Product Description
Elevate sleep hygiene with our premium Anti Allergy Mattress Protector, expertly designed to shield your mattress from spills, allergens, and wear while delivering cloud-like softness. Featuring a triple-layer quilted design, this protector combines a breathable cotton-blend surface for plush comfort with a lab-certified TPU waterproof backing that blocks liquids, sweat, and stains. Its hypoallergenic, OEKO-TEX® certified fabric actively resists dust mites, mold spores, and bacteria, making it ideal for allergy sufferers, asthma patients, or households with kids and pets. The 18-inch deep pocket elastic skirt ensures a no-slip, silent fit on mattresses up to 18" thick (memory foam, hybrid, or adjustable beds), while the noise-free waterproof core eliminates disruptive crinkling sounds. Machine-washable for effortless care, it retains shape and softness through 100+ washes, offering durable protection that aligns with 10-year mattress warranties.
